The APPRECIATE event features research presentations and technology exhibits over two days. Started in 2016, it celebrates groundbreaking student projects from TUP Manila's College of Engineering.

Made Possible by Our Partners

About

The Technological University of the Philippines prioritizes excellence in engineering and related disciplines such as automation, communication, multimedia technology, and manufacturing. Organized by the Civil Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering departments, APPRECIATE is a two-day event showcasing cutting-edge research and insights in the field. Through sessions like Plenary Lectures and Research Presentations, students benefit from valuable career insights provided by industry experts.

Frequently Asked Questions | Trivia

What is Appreciate for?

APPRECIATE stands as a key hub for graduating engineering students, offering them a prime stage to showcase their project research. Not just limited to local exposure, our initiative extends the opportunity for national recognition, appreciating and highlighting the innovative contributions of these aspiring engineers.

Did you know?

During APPRECIATE 2016, we had the honor of being joined by Former Senator Bam Aquino. Hist participation added a significant and insightful dimension to the APPRECIATE 2016 event, enriching the experience for all attendees.

Did you know?

Since its first installment in 2016, APPRECIATE gave a platform to young innovators from TUP - Manila to present their quality research and project studies. Several studies made it to the Philippine Association of Engineering Schools Undergraduate Engineering Design Contest where they bagged awards for their presentation.
