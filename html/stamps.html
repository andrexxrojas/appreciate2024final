Appreciate 2024 Stamp Card

Welcome to the Appreciate 2024 Stamp Card! Earn stamps by attending presentations, pitching sessions, parallel sessions, and visiting exhibits and booths. After each activity, you'll receive a unique code to input into the provided button. Each code counts as a stamp on your digital card. The more activities you participate in, the more stamps you collect! Keep track of your progress and enjoy the event. Thank you for joining us in APPRECIATE 2024!

Pitching / Presentation

To earn stamps in the Pitching/Presentation sector, attend either the Pitching session on Day 1 at the TUP Court or the Presentation on Day 2 at IRTC Hall. You'll receive one stamp code for whichever you attend. Keep enjoying the event and collecting those stamps!

Parallel Sessions

To collect stamps in the Parallel Sessions sector, attend sessions on Day 1 in the COE (College of Engineering) Rooms. Your attendance earns you a stamp. Stay engaged, participate actively, and take notes during the session. Afterward, receive a unique code from the presenter or session organizer. Keep attending sessions to gather stamps and enhance your event experience!

Exhibit and Booths

To earn stamps in the Exhibit and Booths sector, go to TUP Court on Day 1 or Day 2. Interact with exhibits, participate in activities, and collect your unique code for a stamp. Ask questions, explore new technologies, and maybe win prizes! Remember to collect stamps for different engineering courses, be sure to visit the specific engineering-themed booths and exhibits. Enjoy the experience!
